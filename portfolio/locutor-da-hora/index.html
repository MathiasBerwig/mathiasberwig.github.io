Locutor da Hora

The Locutor da Hora is part of the project Rádio, Tecnologias e Empreendedorismo na Escola (Radio, Technology and Entrepreneurship at the School - Facebook page), an extension program initiative of the Regional University of Northwestern Rio Grande do Sul (UNIJUÍ). The app (download) and blog were developed in the period when I had a university extension scholarship, between March of 2013 and March of 2015. Its source code is available on GitHub.
