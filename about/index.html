About me

My name is Mathias Berwig, I’m 20 years old and I’m the type of developer who cares to use the best practices to develop great products and services. I study Computer Science and work as programmer at UNIJUÍ, a university in the northwest of the state of Rio Grande do Sul (Brazil). I also work as a freelance developing softwares or as IT consultant. When I’m not working, I like to get inspired on CodePen and MaterialUp, or getting out to get some beer.

I can help you developing projects of Android or multiplatform applications. My goal as a developer is to build practical solutions with functional, beautiful and responsive interfaces, combining excellent user experience at the best possible performance.

You can see some of my work down in my portfolio or in my GitHub profile. I’m always looking for good opportunities to work with great people on amazing projects. Get in touch.
